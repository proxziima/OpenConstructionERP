DEPRECATED — openconstructionerp-aus-nzs
==========================================

This combined AU + NZ pack is no longer maintained. AU and NZ use
different currencies (AUD vs NZD), different GST rates (10% vs 15%)
and substantially different contract / building-code regimes that
cannot reasonably share a single PartnerPackManifest default.

Migrate to one of:

  pip install openconstructionerp-aus   # Australia
  pip install openconstructionerp-nzs   # New Zealand

The aus-nzs shim re-exports the openconstructionerp-aus manifest
unchanged for backward compatibility. It will be removed in a future
major release.
