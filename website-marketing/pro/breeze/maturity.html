About the bands. The five bands are a self-assessment rubric, not a population benchmark — we don't publish industry-wide percentages because we don't have honest data to back them. The score reflects only what you answered today; the value is in the per-dimension breakdown, not the label.

Time to take it. ~5 minutes for the 11 questions, ~10 minutes if you read the rationale on each option. Re-take it every 6 months — the per-dimension deltas are usually more interesting than the band itself.

What to do next. Pick the two lowest dimensions from the breakdown and treat them as next quarter's focus. Most firms move one band on a single dimension within 12–18 months when they pick a concrete initiative (e.g. "switch BOQ from Excel to a structured tool" rather than "improve digital maturity").
